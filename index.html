Operation Homefront Priorities

A targeted, zero-to-low-cost tactical intervention to hold Congressman Derrick Van Orden accountable in WI-03.

1 Strategic Overview and Mission Mandate

Operation Homefront Priorities is a targeted, zero-to-low-cost tactical intervention designed to hold Congressman Derrick Van Orden accountable within Wisconsin’s 3rd District. Our mission is to execute a strategic pivot from digital mobilization to physical, real-world accountability. This transition is essential to move the needle with swing voters, working families, and veterans who feel abandoned by a legislative focus on $200 billion in foreign war expenditures while local economic stability erodes.

Central Guiding Narrative

"Wisconsin Families First. Not Foreign Wars."

This message serves as the lens through which we frame every grievance, from gas prices to VA care.

This manual dictates a four-phase cumulative strategy. Phase 1 through Phase 3 represent the "softening" of the target before we transition to the "kinetic" impact of Phase 4.

2 Phase 1: Foundational Infrastructure

Speed and security are our primary advantages. We will operate a "zero-to-low-cost" internal infrastructure to maintain a competitive edge against the incumbent’s corporate funding. No public action is to be taken until the command structure is locked.

Internal Command

  • Central Hub: All personnel are directed to a primary workspace (Slack, Discord, or private Facebook Group) for real-time tasking.
  • Digital Toolkit: All assets must be hosted on a central Google Drive. This ensures immediate deployment of "ammunition" without version delays.

Recruitment Command

Recruitment must be localized and relatable. Use "economic" language, not "political".

  • Deployment: Send "Message 1" via personal email and Nextdoor.
  • Engagement: Post pitch in moderate/progressive groups.
  • Onboarding: Conduct a mandatory 15-minute virtual kickoff meeting to distribute first actions.

3 Phase 2: Content & Asset Prep

We cut through national noise by utilizing visual "receipts"—irrefutable data points that ground national spending in local loss. All assets are to be stored in the Digital Toolkit.

Audio, Video, and Written "Ammunition"

  • Leaked Audio: Deploy clipped audio of the incumbent regarding the childcare crisis to underscore domestic neglect.
  • Video Templates: Organizers are required to record "Green Screen/Receipt" sample videos for the "Make it Make Sense" challenge.
  • Written Assets: Deploy pre-written templates for Facebook boards and LTEs formatted for the La Crosse Tribune and Leader-Telegram.

4 Phase 3: Guerilla Distribution

Guerilla distribution involves injecting our narrative into non-political digital spaces to reach voters where they live.

Action Set A: The Facebook Infiltration

Focus on "Buy/Sell/Trade" boards. Deploy "Gas Price Vent" and "Childcare Crisis" posts paired with the "Van Orden Tax" graphic. Frame political choices as personal financial burdens.

Action Set B: Short-Form Video Push

Launch the "Make it Make Sense" challenge on Reels/TikTok. Algorithm Training: Volunteers are strictly forbidden from using national hashtags. You must use: #WI03 #EauClaire #LaCrosse #WisconsinPolitics.

Action Set C: LTE Blitz

Dominate local print media. Goal: A steady drumbeat of 2-3 published pieces per week across the district.

5 Phase 4: Tactical Field Escalation

Digital momentum must now be converted into physical accountability. We will use the candidate’s own public appearances to generate viral "earned media." Maintain a centralized "Tracker Spreadsheet" for all public appearances.

Bird-Dogging Protocol

Deploy specialized Two-Person Teams for every encounter:

  • 1. The Interrogator: Armed with a pointed question. You must use the mandatory script: "Why fund a $200B war while cutting WI-03 VA care?"
  • 2. The Videographer: Positioned to capture reaction. Vertical recording on a smartphone is a requirement for mobile-first platforms.

6 Rapid Amplification

In the 24-hour news cycle, field footage is useless unless amplified instantly. This creates a "seamless loop" where physical encounters feed back into digital content.

Identify & Edit

Isolate 15s evasive segments. Add clear captions.

Distribute

Tag local journalists on X immediately.

Seed

Post to r/wisconsin and r/lacrossewi.
