Community Board Posts (6 Options)

1. The Gas Price Shock
Just paid an absolute fortune to fill up my car here in town. 🤦‍♂️ I really don't get it—we are getting crushed by costs here in Wisconsin, but Congressman Van Orden is voting to send $200 BILLION to the Middle East for a new war. That war is driving up oil prices, and we are footing the bill. Make it make sense. We need a rep who puts our economy first.
2. The VA/Healthcare Trade
It’s frustrating watching rural hospitals and VA clinics struggle to stay fully funded in Western Wisconsin. What’s worse is knowing Rep. Van Orden previously backed cuts to VA healthcare... but suddenly has no problem finding $200 BILLION to fund a massive new war in Iran. Our veterans and local communities should come before overseas wars. Period.
3. The Childcare Squeeze
Is anyone else dealing with insane daycare waitlists and costs around the Chippewa Valley? 🙋‍♀️ It really stung to see the recent news where politicians admitted they can't fund childcare because they have to pay for the new war in the Middle East—a war Derrick Van Orden is backing with our tax dollars. Our kids are taking a backseat to bombs.
4. The Grocery Bill Shock
Just left Woodman's and my jaw is on the floor looking at this receipt. 🧾 Groceries are out of control. Meanwhile, Congressman Van Orden is in Washington voting to send $200,000,000,000 to the Middle East for a new war that is only going to drive global inflation higher. Why are we funding a forever war when we can barely afford to feed our families here in Wisconsin?
5. Main Street Betrayal
We hear a lot about "America First," but when you look at Derrick Van Orden's voting record, it's actually "Middle East Wars First." He’s voting to dump $200 billion into a war in Iran instead of investing in infrastructure, broadband, or small businesses right here in the Driftless Area. He’s draining resources from Wisconsin Main Streets to pay for DC's foreign policy mistakes.
6. The Rubber Stamp
I voted for change, not a rubber stamp for DC War Hawks. Derrick Van Orden promised to stop the endless wars. Now he’s backing a $200 billion blank check for a new conflict in Iran. Whatever happened to keeping our troops home and focusing on the economy? He broke his biggest promise to voters. 🚫

Field Q&A Scripts (6 Questions)

Rules: Go in pairs. One records vertical video, one asks the question. Be polite, don't yell. Let his reaction speak for itself.

1. Gas & Living Costs

"Congressman Van Orden, gas and groceries are incredibly expensive for families here. How does sending $200 billion of our tax dollars to fight a war in Iran help my family afford to live in Wisconsin?"

2. Veteran Hypocrisy

"Congressman, as a veteran yourself, how do you justify finding $200 billion to start a new war in the Middle East, while backing cuts to VA healthcare for the veterans living right here in your district?"

3. Childcare Trade-Off

"Congressman, the administration recently said we can't afford childcare funding because we have to pay for the war in Iran. Do you agree with that? Why are foreign wars more important than Wisconsin families?"

4. Grocery Inflation

"Congressman, my grocery bill has skyrocketed, and economists say the Middle East conflict is making inflation worse. Why are you voting to spend $200 billion to escalate a war that is directly raising food prices for your constituents?"

5. Rural Hospitals

"Congressman, rural hospitals across our state are struggling to keep their doors open. Why is Congress able to instantly find $200 billion for bombs and missiles overseas, but leaves our local ERs and nurses begging for scraps?"

6. "America First" Promise

"Congressman, you campaigned heavily on the 'America First' agenda and promised to stop the endless wars. Since you are now backing a $200 billion war in Iran, do you admit you broke your promise to put America First?"
